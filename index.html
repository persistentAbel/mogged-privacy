Privacy Policy for Mogged

Last Updated: March 11, 2026
Developer: Arturo Gomez
Contact: iosabeldev@gmail.com

Mogged ("the App") is a screen time discipline app that locks distracting apps and requires users to complete physical exercises (Mog Sets) to unlock them. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Mogged does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, emails, or phone numbers
  • Account or login information
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, videos, or device files
  • Advertising identifiers or analytics data
  • Any sensitive personal information
  • Facial data, biometric data, or camera input

2. Screen Time & App Usage Data

Mogged uses Apple's Screen Time framework (FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, DeviceActivityMonitor) to lock and unlock apps selected by the user. This data is processed entirely on-device by Apple's operating system. Mogged does not have access to, transmit, or store any information about which apps you use or how long you use them.

3. Local Storage

Mogged stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults (App Groups), shared only between the app and its system extensions:

  • Your current streak count
  • Total Mog Sets completed
  • Dates of completed Mog Sets (for calendar display)
  • Unlock window timestamps
  • Your first name (entered during onboarding, used for personalization only)

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

4. Purchases & Subscriptions

Mogged uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect purchase-related data (such as subscription status and transaction IDs) in accordance with their own privacy policy. No payment information is ever handled or stored by Mogged directly. All purchases are processed by Apple.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Third-Party Services

Mogged does not integrate with any advertising networks, analytics platforms, or third-party tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

Mogged is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
